Simplifying (by Recognizing “1”) when dividing—

I. $\frac{{30{x^6}{y^9}}}{{15{x^6}{y^5}}} = \frac{{2 \cdot 15{x^6}{y^5}{y^4}}}{{15{x^6}{y^5}}}\\ = \frac{2}{1} \cdot \frac{{15{x^6}{y^5}}}{{15{x^6}{y^5}}} \cdot \frac{{{y^4}}}{1}\\ = \frac{{2{y^4}}}{1}\\ = 2{y^4} $
